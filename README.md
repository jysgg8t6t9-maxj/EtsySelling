# My Budget — Your Private Personal Budget App

A clean, app-like personal budgeting tool that runs entirely in your browser.
Track income and spending, plan a monthly budget, crush debt and hit savings
goals — with everything saved **privately on your own device**.

> 🔒 **Your data never leaves your device.** There's no account, no sign-up and
> no server. Everything you enter is stored only in your browser, on your
> computer or phone. Only you can see it.

---

## ⚡ Quick start (the easy way)

1. **Unzip** this folder.
2. **Double-click `index.html`.** It opens in your web browser and works
   immediately — no install, no internet required.
3. Click **Settings ▸ Load sample** to explore with example data, then
   **Clear all data** when you're ready to start fresh with your own numbers.

That's it. Add a transaction, set your budget, and watch the dashboard update.

> 💡 **Tip:** bookmark the page so it's one tap away. On a phone, open it in your
> browser and choose **“Add to Home Screen”** — it then behaves like a real app
> with its own icon.

## 🌐 Make it a private website (host it on GitHub Pages — free)

Want to open your app from any device at your own web address? Host your own
free copy in about 3 minutes:

1. Create a free account at **github.com**.
2. Click **New repository**, give it a name (e.g. `my-budget`), set it to
   **Public**, and create it.
3. Click **Add file ▸ Upload files**, drag in `index.html`, `styles.css` and
   `app.js`, and commit.
4. Go to **Settings ▸ Pages**. Under *Build and deployment ▸ Branch*, choose
   **main** and **/(root)**, then **Save**.
5. Wait ~1 minute and refresh. GitHub shows your live link, e.g.
   `https://yourname.github.io/my-budget/` — open it anywhere.

Because your data is stored per-device, each browser you open it in keeps its
own private copy. Use **Export / Import** (below) to move your data between
devices.

## 🔑 Optional passcode lock

Go to **Settings ▸ Privacy lock ▸ Set passcode** to add a lock screen when the
app opens. This is a friendly privacy lock for shared devices — not bank-grade
security — and your data always stays on your device either way.
*Forgot your passcode?* See **Reset** at the bottom of this guide.

---

## 💾 Backing up & moving your data

Your data lives in one browser. To keep it safe or move it to a new
phone/computer:

- **Settings ▸ Export data** downloads a small backup file.
- On the other device, open the app and choose **Settings ▸ Import data**.

Back up now and then — clearing your browser data will erase the app's memory.

---

## 🎨 Make it yours

- **Settings ▸ Personalise** changes the app name, tagline and currency symbol.
- Want to recolor it? Open `styles.css` and edit the colours at the very top
  (the `:root` block). Change `--brand` and `--accent` and the whole app
  re-themes.

---

## ❓ FAQ

**Does it work offline?** Yes. Once the files are on your device it needs no
internet.

**Does it work on my phone?** Yes — it's fully responsive with its own mobile
layout and bottom menu.

**Is my data sent anywhere?** No. There is no server and no tracking. Everything
stays in your browser's local storage.

**Reset / forgot passcode:** open the app, and in your browser console
(or by clearing this site's data in browser settings) the app resets. If you
have a backup file, you can re-import it afterwards.
